Make your own Chocolate Frappe!

Status:
0%
Place 1-1 and 1/2 cups of ice in the blender first.
Pour in 1 cup cold milk.
Add 1–2 tbsp chocolate syrup, 1 tsp instant coffee (optional), and 1–2 tsp sugar (optional).
Blend on high for 30–45 seconds, if too thick, add a splash of milk; if too thin, add more ice.
Add whipped cream and drizzle chocolate syrup it and enjoy!

Bonus: Freeze milk into ice cubes to keep the frappe creamy without diluting the flavor when blending.
