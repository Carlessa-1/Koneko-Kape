Make your own Caramel Macchiato!

Status:
0%
Pour 1–2 tbsp of vanilla syrup into your serving glass or cup.
Steam 1-1 and 1/2 cups of milk (65–70°C) and pour gently over the syrup.
Add 1–2 shots of espresso.
Spoon a layer of milk foam on top. (optional)
Drizzle caramel syrup over the foam and enjoy!

Bonus: Use whole milk or oat milk for creaminess!
