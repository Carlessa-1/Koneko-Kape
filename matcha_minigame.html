Make your own Matcha!

Status:
0%
Add 1-2 teaspoons of matcha powder into a small mug or bowl.
Sift the matcha to avoid any lumps.
Pour in 2 oz of hot water.
Whisk briskly from side to side until the matcha is fully dispersed and there is a foamy layer on top.
Add in 6 oz. of hot water or steamed milk and whisk again until foamy.

Bonus: Try adding maple syrup, or honey for a sweeter drink. Enjoy your matcha!
